Radio group with error - aria-label applied to <input>

← Return to main list

This is a test case designed to determine how an error message associated with a radio group will be announced by various screen readers.

Example

Do you like boats?

Error: Choose at least one option

Code

<fieldset>
  <legend>Do you like boats?</legend>
  <div class="form-group">
    <input type="radio" id="boats-y" name="boats" aria-label="Yes. Error: Choose at least one option">
    <label for="boats-y">Yes</label>
  </div>
  <div class="form-group">
    <input type="radio" id="boats-n" name="boats" aria-label="No. Error: Choose at least one option">
    <label for="boats-n">No</label>
  </div>
  <p class="error-message" id="e01">Error: Choose at least one option</p>
</fieldset>

Assistive technologies

VoiceOver

  • Chrome: Yes. Error: Choose at least one option. Radio button. 1 of 2. Do you like boats? Group.
  • Firefox: Yes. Error: Choose at least one option. Radio button. 1 of 2. Do you like boats? Group.
  • Safari: Yes. Error: Choose at least one option. Radio button. 1 of 2. Do you like boats? Do you like boats? Group. [Legend announced twice]

NVDA

  • Chrome: Do you like boats? Grouping. Yes. Error: Choose at least one option. Radio button. Not checked. 1 of 2.
  • Firefox: Do you like boats? Grouping. Yes. Error: Choose at least one option. Radio button. Not checked. 1 of 2..
  • Edge: Do you like boats? Grouping. Yes. Error: Choose at least one option. Radio button. Not checked. 1 of 2.

JAWS

  • Chrome: Do you like boats? Group. Yes. Error: Choose at least one option. Radio button. Not checked. 1 of 2.
  • Firefox: To change the selection use the up or down arrow. Do you like boats? Yes. Error: Choose at least one option. Radio button. Not checked. Yes. Error: Choose at least one option. Radio button. Not checked. 1 of 2.
  • Edge: Do you like boats? Group. Yes. Error: Choose at least one option. Radio button. Not checked. 1 of 2.
