This is a test case designed to determine how the <input type="date"> element is announced by various screen readers.

Example

Code

<div>
  <label for="date">Choose a date</label>
  <input type="date" id="date">
</div>

Assistive technologies

VoiceOver

  • Chrome: Day. Choose a date. Stepper. DD.
  • Firefox: Day. Stepper.
  • Safari: Choose a date. Edit text.

NVDA

  • Chrome: Choose a date. Date edit. Day. Choose a date. Spin button. 0.
  • Firefox: Choose a date. Date edit. Day. Spin button. 50.
  • Edge: Choose a date. Date edit. Day. Choose a date. Spin button. 0.

JAWS

  • Chrome: Choose a date. Date time edit. Day. Choose a date. Spin button. 0.
  • Firefox: Choose a date. Date time edit. Day. Spin button. 50.
  • Edge: Choose a date. Date time edit. Day. Choose a date. Spin button. 0.
